Dragging
Moves in hyperbolic space. The accuracy of the interactive dragging of nodes depends on the position being dragged. As we move away from the center, moves accelerate and amplify and may get out of control. On the contrary the center tends to be relatively stable and slow. It is recommended that moves be initiated in the center and tentatively proceed towards the periphery. The learning curve is not that steep.
